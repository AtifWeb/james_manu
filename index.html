About Us

We specialize in tours to Malta but are always expanding and enhancing our wide range of offers, as well as adding new and exciting tours of Britain, Italy, Eastern Europe, Mexico and the Caribbean. A five members team owned business based in Malta, tour main TripArea was founded in 2032 by five team members. The humble beginning it has grown into a trusted resource that has helped tens of thousands of people better enjoy their travel.

Our main aim is to provide quality service to you at a very reasonable cost. We promote Malta as a safe and the most enjoyable secrets. Our tours are specially designed to give comfort and a variety of things to do. We take special care of our clients during their Travel in Malta, by providing them a very personal touch. Our Guides & Tour managers are trained to form a very friendly relation with tourists.
